## 第五步：五点描述

### Point 1: Designed for Outdoor Sunlight
**Made with UV-resistant materials to help reduce fading in patio, porch and garden use.**

> Designed for outdoor placement where direct sun is common. The UV-resistant treatment helps petals retain color longer than standard faux florals, which often fade within weeks. Suitable for patio, porch, and garden arrangements that receive full sun exposure.

### Point 2: Realistic Color and Layered Petals
**Natural color variation and fuller flower heads create a lifelike look from a distance.**

> Each bloom uses layered petals with subtle color variation to mimic real garden flowers. Unlike flat single-layer faux florals, the fuller heads hold their shape and create depth, so arrangements look natural from the curb.

### Point 3: Perfect for Porch, Patio and Garden Planters
**Flexible stems insert directly into planters, baskets and porch boxes.**

> Each bundle has bendable stems with internal wire, allowing you to shape them naturally. Insert directly into soil, foam, or planter filler — no tools, no assembly, no gardening gloves required. Ideal for porch railings, patio urns, window boxes, and garden containers.

### Point 4: 12 Bundles for Full Arrangements
**Each set includes 12 bundles, enough to fill 2-3 medium planters or one long window box.**

> Bundle count is calibrated for typical outdoor displays. Each bundle measures approximately 14-16 inches tall, suitable for 10-14 inch planters. For larger containers or lush fullness, combine 2 sets.

### Point 5: Protective Packaging and Maintenance-Free
**Shipped with reinforced packaging. No watering, no trimming, no seasonal replanting.**

> Each set is packed with internal supports to reduce petal crushing during transit. If a bundle arrives misshapen, gentle fluffing restores the shape in minutes. Once arranged, no maintenance needed — keeps outdoor spaces colorful through spring, summer, and fall.

## 第八步：QA（10 题）

### Q1: Will these artificial flowers fade in direct sunlight?
A: They are made with UV-resistant materials to help reduce fading during outdoor use, but like all outdoor decor, long-term extreme sun exposure may gradually affect color. For best longevity, shelter during severe weather.

### Q2: Can I use them in outdoor planters?
A: Yes, the flexible stems can be inserted directly into planters, pots, baskets and porch boxes. Each bundle is shaped to mimic real blooms.

### Q3: Are the stems flexible enough to bend?
A: Yes, the stems are made of durable plastic with internal wire, allowing you to shape them naturally and fit different planter sizes.

### Q4: How many bundles do I need for a medium planter?
A: For a 12-inch medium planter, 4-6 bundles create full coverage. The 12-bundle set fills 2-3 medium planters or one long window box.

### Q5: Do they look realistic up close?
A: Natural color variation and layered petals create a lifelike look from a distance. For very close inspection, the petals may feel slightly plastic to the touch.

### Q6: Will they fall apart or shed?
A: The bundles are assembled with reinforced attachment points to reduce shedding during normal handling. Gentle fluffing after unboxing restores the natural shape.

### Q7: Can I leave them outside in rain?
A: Yes, the materials are water-resistant and suitable for outdoor use in normal weather. For longest life, we recommend sheltering during heavy storms or extreme winds.

### Q8: How tall are they?
A: Each bundle measures approximately 14-16 inches tall, suitable for medium and large planters.

### Q9: Will the color look fake or too bright?
A: The petals use natural color variation to avoid the over-saturated look common in cheap faux florals. Colors are designed to mimic real garden blooms.

### Q10: Is the packaging protective?
A: Each set is shipped with reinforced packaging to reduce transit damage. If any bundle arrives misshapen, gentle fluffing restores the shape.
