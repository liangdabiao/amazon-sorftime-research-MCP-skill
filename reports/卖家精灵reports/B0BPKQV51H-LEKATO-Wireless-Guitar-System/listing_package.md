### 五点描述

**🔊 5.8GHz Anti-Interference — Say Goodbye to 2.4GHz Dropouts**
Unlike most budget wireless guitar systems stuck on the crowded 2.4GHz band (where WiFi, Bluetooth, and neon lights constantly cause static and dropouts), LEKATO's upgraded 5.8GHz band operates on a cleaner, uncompressed frequency. Whether you're gigging in a crowded venue, leading worship at church, or recording in a WiFi-saturated home studio, you'll get a rock-solid, dropout-free signal — every single time.

**🎵 24-Bit / 48KHz Lossless Audio — Your Tone, Unchanged**
Hear your tone exactly as your pickups deliver it. With 24-bit / 48KHz uncompressed broadcasting and under 6ms latency (you literally cannot perceive it), every nuance of your shredding, fingerpicking, and slapping comes through crystal clear. 100-foot line-of-sight range means you can roam the stage, walk into the crowd, or pace the studio — without losing a single high or low. **No compression. No tone coloration. No muffled sound.**

**🎸 220° Rotatable Plug — Fits Strat, Les Paul, Bass & More**
Engineered to fit nearly every 1/4" input instrument — Fender Stratocasters with recessed jacks, Gibson Les Pauls, electric-acoustics, and **both active and passive basses** (yes, your low end stays full and round). The 220° swivel stays out of the way of your picking hand and sits flush on any pedalboard. **Note for high-output humbuckers (>15kΩ, e.g. Seymour Duncan Distortion):** a slight clean-channel overdrive may occur — pair with an attenuator for pristine tone.

**🔋 5-Hour Battery + Dual USB — Survive Any Gig, Any Rehearsal**
Built-in rechargeable lithium batteries deliver up to 5 hours of continuous playtime — enough for a 3-hour gig plus two encores, a full worship service cycle, or a long studio session. The included 2-in-1 dual USB cable charges transmitter and receiver simultaneously from any power bank, laptop, or wall adapter. **A clear battery indicator warns you before silence — no mid-song surprises.** (Tip: For bands doing 4+ hour rehearsals, a 10-min top-up between sets keeps you going all night.)

**⚡ Plug & Play, 4 Channels — Less Cable, More Music**
Zero setup, zero learning curve. Long-press the signal key once — transmitter and receiver auto-pair and remember each other for next time. 4 discrete channels let you and up to 3 bandmates go wireless on the same stage with zero cross-talk or audio bleed. Comes in a road-ready, padded-gig-bag-friendly size (6.69" × 4.33" × 1.57", 10.6 oz) backed by LEKATO's responsive US seller support. **Why pay premium prices when 90% of gigging guitarists can't hear the difference?**
