## 四、QA 草稿（10 个 Alexa/Rufus 对话式问答）

### Q1: 干扰（最高频疑虑）
**Q:** Will this wireless guitar system have interference or dropouts in a venue with lots of WiFi and phones?  
**A:** No. LEKATO uses the **5.8GHz band** — deliberately separate from the crowded 2.4GHz band where WiFi, Bluetooth, and phones operate. In real-world testing across 250-seat ballrooms, multi-router churches, and home studios, the signal stays clean. If you ever do hit interference (rare), 4 switchable channels let you dodge it in seconds.

### Q2: 拾音器兼容
**Q:** Does it work with active pickups or high-output humbuckers like Seymour Duncan Distortion?  
**A:** Yes for active pickups. For very hot humbuckers above ~15kΩ (Seymour Duncan Distortion, some EMGs), you may notice a faint clean-channel push — pair with a small attenuator for absolute pristine tone.

### Q3: 乐器兼容
**Q:** Will this work for my Fender Stratocaster with a recessed input jack?  
**A:** Yes. The 220° rotatable plug is specifically designed for recessed Strat jacks, Gibson Les Pauls, Telecasters, electric-acoustics, and both active and passive basses. The swivel keeps it flush against your guitar body — no cable sticking out to bump.

### Q4: 贝斯
**Q:** Can I use this for a bass guitar? Will it lose the low end?  
**A:** Yes. Unlike cheap 2.4GHz systems that compress low frequencies, LEKATO's 24-bit/48KHz uncompressed audio preserves the full frequency range — including the deep roundness bassists need. Works with both active and passive basses.

### Q5: 电池
**Q:** How long does the battery last on a full charge? Can I use it while charging?  
**A:** Up to **5 hours of continuous playtime** per charge — enough for a 3-hour gig or full worship service. The included 2-in-1 dual USB cable charges both units at once. You cannot play while charging, but a 10-minute top-up between sets adds ~1 hour of runtime.

### Q6: 多通道
**Q:** Can my whole band use these at the same time without interference?  
**A:** Yes — LEKATO supports **4 simultaneous channels**, so you and up to 3 bandmates can each use a paired set on the same stage with zero cross-talk or audio bleed. Each pair auto-pairs on its own channel.

### Q7: 距离
**Q:** What's the wireless range? Will it cover a large stage?  
**A:** Up to **100 feet line-of-sight** — enough to roam any stage, walk to the mixing desk, or step into the crowd. Walls, bodies, and metal racks will reduce range; for outdoor festivals we recommend staying within 60 ft of your amp.

### Q8: 设置难度
**Q:** Is this plug-and-play or do I need to configure anything?  
**A:** Truly plug-and-play. Long-press the signal key on both units for 2 seconds — they auto-pair and remember each other for next time. No apps, no drivers, no setup screens.

### Q9: 质保
**Q:** What's the warranty and how does LEKATO handle returns?  
**A:** LEKATO backs this with a **12-month worry-free warranty** through their responsive US-based seller. Verified buyers report hassle-free replacements for manufacturing defects. For Amazon returns, the standard 30-day window applies.

### Q10: 西语市场（对话式覆盖）
**Q:** ¿Funciona con guitarra acústica-eléctrica y bajo activo?  
**A:** Sí. El sistema LEKATO de 5.8GHz es compatible con guitarras eléctricas, guitarras electroacústicas, bajos activos y pasivos, y la mayoría de instrumentos con entrada de 1/4". El enchufe giratorio de 220° se adapta a Stratocasters con jack empotrado.
